Place your Inter font files here so the app uses your uploaded font instead of Google Fonts.

Expected filenames (rename your .ttf files to match if needed):
  - Inter-Regular.ttf   (weight 400)
  - Inter-Medium.ttf   (weight 500, optional)
  - Inter-SemiBold.ttf (weight 600, optional)
  - Inter-Bold.ttf     (weight 700)
  - Inter-ExtraBold.ttf (weight 800, optional)
  - Inter-Thin.ttf     (weight 100, optional)

Minimum: Inter-Regular.ttf and Inter-Bold.ttf. The app will use the closest weight for others.

For server-side (FFmpeg) export, also place fonts in: server/assets/fonts/
  Use the same names, or: Inter_18pt-Regular.ttf, Inter_18pt-Bold.ttf, Inter_18pt-Thin.ttf
